Frequently Asked Questions

How much can we save with Symphony PM?
On average, teams save 40% of dev time previously spent on coordination and context-switching. For a 10-person team at $150K/dev salary, that's ~$600K in recovered productivity annually. Typical ROI payback is 2-3 months.
Can we upgrade or downgrade our plan?
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle. We'll prorate any differences.
Do you offer discounts for annual billing?
Yes. Annual plans get 20% off. Growth plan: $14,400/year (instead of $18,000). Starter plan: $4,800/year (instead of $6,000).
What happens when we add more developers?
Starter and Growth plans have developer seat limits. If you exceed your plan's limit, you'll be prompted to upgrade to the next tier. Enterprise customers have unlimited seats.
Is there a free trial?
Yes. All plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.
What support do you offer?
Starter plans get email support (24-hour response). Growth plans get priority support (2-hour response). Enterprise customers get a dedicated account manager and 24/7 phone support.
